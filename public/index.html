WELCOME TO IRAS!

Become a guardian of your community with IRAS. Report incidents as they happen and explore real-time data analytics to stay informed. Together, we transform scattered reports into actionable insights for a safer environment.

What is IRAS?

The Incident Report and Awareness System (IRAS) is a crowdsourcing platform designed to document and visualize community incidents—from hazards and accidents to local crimes. By aggregating user reports into an interactive dashboard, IRAS helps citizens identify hotspots and track safety trends over time. Please note: IRAS is a data awareness tool, not a replacement for emergency hotlines.

How It Works

1

Report & Verify

Users securely log in via OTP to submit incidents with precise location pinning, detailed descriptions, and photo evidence.

Our system uses Geocoding to automatically convert your map pin into a readable street address for accuracy.

2

Aggregate & Store

Reports are securely stored in our cloud database, instantly categorizing incidents by type (e.g., Fire, Theft, Road Damage).

The system organizes this crowdsourced data to build a historical record of community safety events.

3

Visualize Trends

Data is instantly visualized on the Analytics Dashboard using interactive Google Charts (Line, Bar, and Pie).

Users can view "Danger Zones" (hotspots) on the map and filter data by date or category to make informed decisions.
